About TianGuanCiFu

Tian Guan Ci Fu is a Chinese Bl Novel written by Mo Xiang Tong Xiu.
It tells the story of one of the gods of the Heavens, Xie Lian, and a mysterious demon king, Hua Cheng.

Story Overview


Xie Lian was the crown prince of the Xianle kingdom. He was loved by his parents, the king and queen of the Xianle kingdom, as well as his people. He ascended to the Heavens at a young age, but due to misfortunates happening in his mortal kingdom, he was bannished back to the mortal realm. Years later, he ascends again, only to be banished minutes after. 800 years later, Xie Lian asends to the Heavens for a third time. On his first mission, he meets the mysterious ghost king who rules all ghosts and terrifies the Heavens. However, their encounter was no accident this ghost king has been paying attention to Xie Lian for a very long time.
