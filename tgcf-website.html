About Tian Guan Ci fu

Tian Guan Ci Fu is a Chinese Bl Novel written by Mo Xiang Tong Xiu.
It tells the story of one of the gods of the Heavens, Xie Lian, and a mysterious demon king, Hua Cheng.

Story Overview

Xie Lian was the crown prince of the Xianle kingdom. He was loved by his parents, the king and queen of the Xianle kingdom, as well as his people.
